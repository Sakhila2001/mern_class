Why Nepal?

About Nepal Trekking

Nepal is home to eight of the world's fourteen eight-thousanders, miles of ancient trading routes, and some of the warmest hospitality on earth. Trekking here isn't just exercise — it's a journey through living culture, dramatic landscapes, and personal transformation.

Everest Region

Trek through Sherpa villages and high-altitude passes toward the iconic Khumbu valley. Reach Everest Base Camp at 5,364 m or summit Kala Patthar for a breathtaking sunrise.

Explore treks →

Annapurna Region

One of the world's great treks — from subtropical forests to the world's deepest gorge and the high-altitude desert of Mustang. The Annapurna Circuit is a bucket-list classic.

Explore treks →

Langtang Region

Nepal's closest major trekking area to Kathmandu. The valley offers glaciers, yak pastures, and the stunning Gosainkunda lake — with far fewer crowds than Everest or Annapurna.

Explore treks →

Mustang & Manaslu

Off the beaten path and utterly captivating. Upper Mustang's ancient walled city and the Manaslu Circuit's dramatic passes offer a raw, restricted-area experience for serious trekkers.

Explore treks →
