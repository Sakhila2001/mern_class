About XYZ College – A Premier Nepal Institution

Established in 1998, XYZ College is one of Nepal’s most respected institutions affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU) and Purbanchal University. Our mission is to foster innovation, critical thinking, and employability. With state-of-the-art labs, a central library with 50k+ books, and collaborations with universities in Japan, Australia, and India, we provide globally competitive education.

We take pride in our 95% placement rate and notable alumni working in Google, Nepali Army, Esewa, and World Bank. XYZ College is not just a campus; it's a launchpad for future leaders.

Admissions 2026 – Join XYZ College Family

Eligibility & Requirements

  • +2 or equivalent (min 45% aggregate) for Bachelor's
  • Entrance Exam conducted by XYZ / TU
  • Documents: transcripts, citizenship, photos, recommendation letter
  • International students: passport & equivalence

Application Process

  1. Fill online application form (free)
  2. Appear for XYZ Entrance Test (June 2026)
  3. Merit list & interview (for scholarships)
  4. Final enrollment & orientation

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the tuition fees for BCA/BIM programs?

Tuition fee ranges from NPR 3.5 Lakhs to 4.2 Lakhs for entire program. Scholarships up to 50% based on entrance merit.

Does XYZ College provide hostel facilities?

Yes, separate modern hostels for boys and girls with Wi-Fi, cafeteria, and 24/7 security.

Is XYZ College affiliated with foreign universities?

We have MoUs with University of Canberra (Australia), Chubu University (Japan), and Purdue University (USA) for student exchange.

How can I apply for a scholarship?

Merit-based, need-based, and sports quota scholarships are available. Fill the scholarship form during admission.
