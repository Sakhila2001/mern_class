About Our College

Rooted in Nepal, reaching for the world

Our Story

Founded in 2001 A.D. (2058 B.S.), Himalaya College of Technology and Management began with a humble vision: to provide accessible, quality higher education to the youth of Nepal. Located in the heart of Kathmandu, our institution has grown from a single program to a comprehensive multi-faculty college serving over 2,000 students annually.

Affiliated to Tribhuvan University and approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of Nepal, we adhere to the highest academic standards while embracing innovation in pedagogy, research, and industry collaboration.

Admissions 2083 B.S. / 2026 A.D.

Your journey to excellence begins here

Himalaya College welcomes applications from students who have completed their +2 (Grade 12) or equivalent examination from any recognized board in Nepal or abroad. Our admissions process is transparent, merit-based, and designed to identify students with genuine potential and passion.

How to Apply — Step by Step

  1. Step 1: Fill the Application Form Download the form from our office or collect it from the admin desk. Fill all details carefully in block letters.
  2. Step 2: Attach Required Documents Compile all required documents as listed below and attach them with the completed application form.
  3. Step 3: Submit & Pay Application Fee Submit the form at the admissions office along with NPR 1,000 application fee (non-refundable).
  4. Step 4: Entrance Examination Appear for the entrance examination on the scheduled date. Admit card will be issued upon successful form submission.
  5. Step 5: Merit List & Counseling Results will be published within 7 working days. Shortlisted students will be called for counseling and seat confirmation.
  6. Step 6: Enrollment & Fee Payment Pay the first semester/year fee and complete enrollment formalities to officially secure your seat.

Eligibility & Required Documents


Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed +2 / Grade 12 from a NEB-recognized institution or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA 2.0 (for management programs) or GPA 2.4 (for technology programs)
  • Must have studied Mathematics (for BCA, BIT, BSc CSIT)
  • Age: Minimum 16 years as of Baishakh 1, 2083 B.S.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form (available at college office)
  • Grade 12 mark sheet (original + 2 photocopies)
  • Grade 12 character certificate
  • SLC / SEE mark sheet and certificate
  • Citizenship certificate or birth certificate (+ 2 copies)
  • 4 passport-sized photographs (recent, white background)
  • Migration certificate (if from outside Bagmati Province)
  • Equivalence certificate (for foreign students)

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We believe financial constraints should never stand in the way of quality education. Himalaya College offers the following scholarships:

  • Merit Scholarship (25% fee waiver): Students scoring GPA 3.6+ in Grade 12
  • Sports Scholarship (20% fee waiver): National or district-level sports achievers
  • Government Quota Scholarships: As per TU and UGC allocation for marginalized communities
  • Staff Dependent Discount (50% fee waiver): Children of permanent Himalaya College staff
  • Sibling Discount (10%): For siblings of currently enrolled students

Campus Facilities

A complete environment designed for learning, growth, and life

Central Library

Our fully air-conditioned library houses over 25,000 books, journals, and digital resources. Open from 7 AM to 8 PM, six days a week, with dedicated reading halls for quiet study.

  • 25,000+ books and journals
  • Digital library & e-resources access
  • 200-seat reading capacity
  • Photocopying and printing services

Computer Laboratories

Six state-of-the-art computer labs equipped with the latest hardware and software. High-speed internet connectivity ensures students have access to global learning resources.

  • 6 labs with 200+ workstations total
  • 100 Mbps fiber internet (UPC Nepal)
  • Licensed software: MS Office, Adobe, IDEs
  • Open access during all college hours

Sports & Recreation

We believe in holistic development. Our sports complex supports multiple disciplines and our teams regularly compete at inter-college and national levels.

  • Cricket & Football ground
  • Basketball & Volleyball courts
  • Indoor badminton hall
  • Table tennis & carrom rooms
  • Fitness center with modern equipment

Cafeteria & Canteen

Our spacious cafeteria serves hygienic, affordable Nepali and continental meals throughout the day. Special dietary options are available for students with specific needs.

  • Capacity: 300 students at a time
  • Nepali, Indian, and continental menu
  • Vegetarian & non-vegetarian options
  • Subsidized meal plans for enrolled students

Student Hostel

Safe, comfortable, and affordable hostel accommodation is available for outstation students. Separate facilities for male and female students with 24-hour security.

  • 150 beds (separate boys/girls blocks)
  • Wi-Fi enabled rooms
  • Attached bathrooms and hot water
  • Warden and 24-hour security
  • Monthly fee: NPR 8,000 (inclusive of meals)

Beyond academics, our campus provides everything needed for a productive and comfortable college experience.

  • Banking counter (NMB Bank)
